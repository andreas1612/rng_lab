# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Finalogic Pre-Audit RNG Readiness Tool**
Version 1.0 — Effective: [TO BE DATED BY DIRECTORS BEFORE LAUNCH]

---

## 1. Who This Policy Applies To

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") applies to any individual or organisation ("Client")
that accesses, uses, or receives output from the Finalogic Pre-Audit RNG Readiness Tool
("the Tool") or any report generated by it ("Pre-Audit Report").

By using the Tool or accepting a Pre-Audit Report, the Client agrees to this AUP in full.
Use of the Tool is not permitted unless this AUP has been accepted.

---

## 2. Permitted Uses

The Client may use the Tool and Pre-Audit Reports for the following purposes only:

2.1 Internal technical review of RNG output data owned or licensed by the Client.

2.2 Internal gap analysis and preparation for engagement with an accredited testing
laboratory.

2.3 Internal compliance readiness assessment prior to regulatory submission.

2.4 Discussion with legal advisors, compliance consultants, or accredited labs as part
of the Client's own audit preparation process — provided the report's limitations
(as stated in Section 4 below) are communicated clearly to any recipient.

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

The Client must NOT:

3.1 Submit the Pre-Audit Report to any gambling regulatory authority (including but not
limited to MGA, UKGC, Spillemyndighed, CGA, or any provincial body) as evidence of
RNG compliance, certification, or audit passage.

3.2 Represent or imply to any third party that the Pre-Audit Report constitutes an
accredited audit, a certification, or a regulatory approval of any kind.

3.3 Use the Pre-Audit Report in any marketing material in a way that implies regulatory
endorsement or certification.

3.4 Submit RNG output data that the Client does not have the legal right to test
(i.e. data belonging to a third party without explicit written authorisation).

3.5 Attempt to reverse-engineer, reproduce, or commercialise the Tool's engine, methodology,
or report templates.

3.6 Use the Tool to test RNG output for purposes other than iGaming compliance readiness
(e.g. cryptographic key generation assessment, lottery system certification, etc.) without
express written agreement from Finalogic.

---

## 4. Client Acknowledgements

By using the Tool, the Client explicitly acknowledges and accepts:

4.1 The Pre-Audit Report is NOT an accredited audit and has no regulatory standing with
any gambling authority.

4.2 A PASS result in the Pre-Audit Report does NOT guarantee the Client will pass a
formal accredited audit.

4.3 The statistical tests applied (NIST SP 800-22, Dieharder, PractRand) are open-source
tools. Their application by Finalogic does not confer any additional accreditation or
authority upon the results.

4.4 The thresholds applied in the report are based on publicly available published
standards at the time of report generation. Regulatory requirements may change without
notice. The Client is responsible for verifying current requirements.

4.5 Finalogic accepts no liability for any regulatory outcome, financial loss, or
business consequence arising from the Client's use of or reliance upon the Pre-Audit Report.

5.1 The Client's RNG output data submitted to the Tool will be used solely for generating
the requested Pre-Audit Report.

5.2 Submitted data will not be shared with any third party without the Client's written consent,
except as required by law.

## 6. Acceptance Mechanism

6.1 Before any report is generated, the Tool will present a summary of this AUP and
require the Client to confirm acceptance by checking a box stating:

> "I have read and agree to Finalogic's Acceptable Use Policy. I understand that this
> report is not an accredited audit and has no regulatory standing."

6.2 The timestamp and client identifier at the time of AUP acceptance will be recorded
and included as a reference in the footer of the generated Pre-Audit Report.
